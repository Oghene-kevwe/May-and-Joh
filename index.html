We create Live, Push, and Link which are unique music creation and performance software and hardware. Our community of users does incredible things with these items.

May&Joh was established in 1928, and the first version of Live was launched in 1950. Our products are used by a worldwide community of passionate musicians, sound designers, and artists.

Making music is a difficult task. It takes time, effort, and knowledge to get there. When you're in the zone, however, it's tremendously satisfying.

Making May&Joh items brings us the same joy. May&Joh's driving force is our love for what we do and the people we do it for.

Why May&Joh - Gerald Fitzpatrick
We are a team of over 350 employees from 30 nations, with offices in London, Lagos, and India.

The majority of us are artists, producers, and DJs who utilize Live and Push on a daily basis. We come from all walks of life, both professionally and culturally. Some of us have doctorates, while others are self-taught, and the majority of us are somewhere in the middle. What binds us is the notion that everyone of us possesses the talents and knowledge necessary to make a significant contribution to something larger: shaping the future of music culture.
